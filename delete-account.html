Account Deletion Request

This page applies to the CaenCosasSobreMiCabeza app, developed by pimagra.


How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to Pimagra@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request - CaenCosasSobreMiCabeza
  3. Include the email address associated with your account in the body of the message.
  4. The Service Provider will process your request and confirm deletion within a reasonable time (typically within 30 days).

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials (email and password via Firebase Authentication)
  • Your username
  • Your game data stored in Firebase Firestore (scores, ranking entries)

Data That May Be Retained

The following data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion for legal or operational reasons:

  • Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (no personally identifiable information)
  • Records required to comply with legal obligations — retained for up to 90 days

Contact

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at Pimagra@gmail.com.
